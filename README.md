# Cross-Selling Marketing Strategy 

## 📌 Project Description
This project focuses on analyzing customer data to improve cross-selling strategies in the insurance and financial services domain. The objective is to identify customer segments, behavioral patterns, and product preferences that help businesses sell additional products to existing customers more effectively.

The analysis is based on customer demographics, income, occupation, family size, education level, lead ratings, and product types to generate actionable marketing insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
- Increase cross-selling conversion rates
- Identify high-potential customer segments
- Optimize product recommendations
- Improve targeted marketing strategies
- Maximize customer lifetime value

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview
- Dataset size: **99,951 records × 15 features**
- Data type: Structured customer & sales data
- Domain: Insurance / Financial Services

### Key Attributes
- Customer demographics (marital status, education, family size)
- Income and occupation
- Lead rating (Cold / Warm / Hot)
- Product type (ANS, TLE, etc.)
- Purchase / conversion indicator

---

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
The following analyses were performed:
- Distribution of lead ratings (Cold, Warm, Hot)
- Marital status impact on product purchase
- Family size vs cross-selling probability
- Product-wise new coverage analysis
- Education-wise conversion rate
- Occupation-wise conversion trends
- Income-based customer insights

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Cold leads form the largest segment and require awareness campaigns and personalized discounts
- Hot leads are fewer but convert fastest and should be prioritized by sales teams
- Married customers show higher purchase potential for family-oriented products
- Customers with 3–5 family members are ideal for bundled insurance plans
- Products **ANS** and **TLE** contribute the highest coverage
- Educated customers have higher conversion rates
- Occupation-based targeting improves cross-selling effectiveness

---

## ✅ Business Recommendations
- Prioritize hot leads for immediate sales conversion
- Run awareness and discount campaigns for cold leads
- Promote family protection and child plans to married customers
- Offer bundled products for mid-size families
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products (ANS & TLE)
- Customize offerings based on education and occupation

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data-driven customer segmentation and behavioral analysis can significantly improve cross-selling performance. By aligning marketing strategies with customer profiles and product preferences, organizations can increase conversions, improve customer satisfaction, and drive revenue growth.
